My favorite poet is...

Ocean
Vuong

Poet, activist, novelist




Born in Saigon, poet and editor Ocean Vuong was raised in Hartford, Connecticut, and earned a BA at Brooklyn College (CUNY). In his poems, he often explores transformation, desire, and violent loss.

— Bio from the Poetry Foundation.





The poem Aubade With Burning City, is from the book Night Sky With Exit Wounds and it is my favorite poem. You can read it here on the Poetry Foundation. Night Sky With Exit Wounds is a collection of poetry by Ocean vuong originally published in 2016. It won the T.S. Elliot Prize in 2017.
